The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Everything Everywhere All at Once

A groundbreaking multiverse adventure that blends sci-fi, action, and deep emotional themes of family and self-discovery. Its innovative narrative structure and visually stunning effects won it widespread acclaim, including multiple Oscars. # Michelle Yeoh performance and the film philosophical exploration of existence, purpose, and love made it a significant cultural milestone.

Wonka

The film captures the magic, imagination, and charm of Roald Dahl universe while appealing to both nostalgic fans and new audiences. Its release was highly anticipated due to its fresh take on a classic character, promising whimsical storytelling combined with deeper themes of ambition, creativity, and kindness.

Barbie

This vibrant and satirical take on the iconic doll transcended its toy origins by engaging with complex themes of gender , identity, and societal expectations. Directed by Greta Gerwig
